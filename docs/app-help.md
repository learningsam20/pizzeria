# Slice of Heaven Pizzeria — App Help

Welcome to the Slice of Heaven ordering and operations platform. This guide explains how to use the app as a **customer**, **staff** member, or **admin**. The **Support Chat** tab uses this same content to answer questions.

---

## Getting started (daily workflow)

| Step | Who | What to do |
|------|-----|------------|
| 1 | Staff / Admin | Sign in — customers can order only while staff is logged in |
| 2 | Customer | Build combos, look up account by **mobile or email**, place order |
| 3 | Staff | **Confirmed → Preparing → Ready → Mark Served → Bill & Pay** |
| 4 | Admin | Review **Analytics** (paid orders) and manage menu, customers, and users |

> **Tip:** Gross sales in Analytics increase only after **Bill & Pay** (order marked delivered), not when the order is first placed.

---

## Customer (dine-in)

- Build combos: **base + pizzas + toppings** (up to 10 pizzas per order)  
- **Look up** your account by mobile **or** email, then confirm or edit **name, phone, email, and address**  
- Your details are **saved or updated** in the customer registry when you place the order  
- Choose a table (or scan a table QR code to have it pre-selected)  
- View **Order History** by order ID, phone, or email  
- Pay at the counter when staff completes **Bill & Pay**  

*A staff or admin member must be signed in before you can place an order.*

---

## Staff (kitchen / front of house)

| Task | Where in the app |
|------|------------------|
| View live order queue | **Staff Kitchen** |
| Filter by status | Open, Confirmed, Preparing, Ready, etc. |
| Start cooking | **Start Preparing** on confirmed orders |
| Mark ready | **Mark Ready** when food is done |
| Mark served | **Mark Served** when food reaches the table *(required before payment)* |
| Collect payment | **Bill & Pay** — choose Cash, Card, or UPI |
| Table QR links | **QR Generator** — share link for dine-in ordering |
| Questions about policy or orders | **Support Chat** |

### Order pipeline

```
Confirmed → Preparing → Ready → Mark Served → Bill & Pay → Delivered
```

| Step | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Confirmed** | Customer placed the order |
| **Preparing** | Kitchen is working on it |
| **Ready** | Ready to bring to the table |
| **Mark Served** | Food delivered to the table |
| **Bill & Pay** | Payment collected; order complete |
| **Cancel** | Only available while order is still **Confirmed** |

## Admin (executive)

Everything staff can do, plus:

| Area | What you can do |
|------|-----------------|
| **Analytics** | Gross sales (paid orders), pipeline value, table usage, popular items, staff performance |
| **Customers** | Search registry, look up by mobile/email, add or update customers, bulk import |
| **Pizza & Master Menu** | Add items, bulk upload, **Reload input_data** after menu file fixes |
| **User Management** | Invite staff or admin by email |
| **Store Settings** | Bulk discount %, minimum pizza qty, currency, GST % |
| **Help** | This guide |

### Inviting team members

**Admin → User Management → Invite User**

1. Enter work email, display name, and role (**Staff** or **Admin**)  
2. Click **Send invitation**  

The new user receives login instructions by **email only** (you will not see their temporary password). On **first login**, they must set a new password.

### Store billing settings

**Admin → Store Settings**

- Bulk discount applies when total **pizza quantity** in an order meets the minimum (default: 5+ pizzas → 10% off subtotal)  
- GST is calculated on the discounted subtotal (default: 5%)  
- Changes apply to **new orders** immediately

## Customer checkout (step by step)

1. Add one or more combos to your cart  
2. Enter mobile **or** email and click **Look up** (or fill details manually for a new account)  
3. Review and edit **name, mobile, email, address**  
4. Confirm your **table**  
5. Click **Place Order** — note your **Order #** for history lookup  

---

## Billing & receipts

- Bill preview shows subtotal, bulk discount (when eligible), GST, and total payable  
- Payment is collected by staff at **Bill & Pay** after the order is **Mark Served**  
- Discount applies to **pizza count** in the order, not bases or toppings alone

## Order history (customers)

**Customer tab → Order History**

- Search by **Order ID**, **phone**, or **email**  
- Filter by order status  
- Track progress: confirmed, preparing, ready, delivered, cancelled

## FAQ

**Why can't I place an order?**  
A staff or admin member must sign in first.

**Why didn't Analytics gross sales update when I placed an order?**  
Revenue counts **paid (delivered)** orders only. Open orders show under pipeline value until staff completes **Bill & Pay**.

**Where is my bulk discount?**  
It applies when your cart has enough **pizzas** (not bases/toppings alone). Admins can check thresholds in **Store Settings**.

**How do I find an old order?**  
Use **Order History** with your order ID, phone, or email.

**Menu items missing or wrong after startup?**  
Ask an admin to use **Admin → Pizza & Master Menu → Reload input_data** or add items manually.

**Can staff cancel an order?**  
Yes, but only while the order is still **Confirmed**.

**How do table QR codes work?**  
Staff generates a QR link for a table. Scanning opens ordering with that table already selected.

**Is email required at checkout?**  
Yes — name, mobile, and email are all required.

**Can I look up customers by email?**  
Yes — at checkout and in **Admin → Customers** using **Look up** with mobile or email.

**Why does the Help page mention reloading analytics?**  
The Analytics tab refreshes automatically while open. Use **Refresh analytics** if numbers look stale after staff completes payments.
